# Questions

## Multiple Choice

**1.** Which of the following is NOT one of the seven elements of art?
- a) Line
- b) Shape
- c) Rhythm
- d) Texture

**2.** Form differs from shape because form has:
- a) Color
- b) Height, width, and depth
- c) Texture
- d) Boundaries

**3.** Which principle of art is created through the repetition of visual elements?
- a) Balance
- b) Rhythm
- c) Emphasis
- d) Proportion

**4.** A gradual transition from one state to another (light to dark, large to small) is called:
- a) Harmony
- b) Gradation
- c) Variety
- d) Rhythm

**5.** Color is composed of three components:
- a) Line, shape, and form
- b) Hue, saturation, and value
- c) Light, dark, and medium
- d) Warm, cool, and neutral

## True or False

**1.** There are seven elements of art and eight principles of art.

**2.** Warm colors recede while cool colors advance toward the viewer.

**3.** Actual texture is physical and tactile, while implied texture is visual.

**4.** Too much variety in a composition creates harmony.

**5.** Movement in art can be literal (depicting motion) or implied (using compositional flow).

## Matching

| Term | Definition |
|------|------------|
| **1.** Line | a) The distribution of visual weight in a composition |
| **2.** Value | b) Marks that connect two points |
| **3.** Texture | c) The lightness or darkness of a color or tone |
| **4.** Balance | d) Creating a focal point through contrast |
| **5.** Emphasis | e) The surface quality of an artwork — how it feels or appears to feel |
