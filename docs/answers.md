## Short Answer

**1. Explain how elements and principles work together.**

Elements and principles are not separate — they interact in every artwork to create meaning. For example, in *The Starry Night*, Van Gogh uses the **element of color** (deep blues and vibrant yellows) together with the **principle of rhythm** (repeated swirling curves) and the **principle of movement** (circular flow across the canvas) to create emotional energy and a sense of a turbulent, living sky.
